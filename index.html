Our Shop Vandalize

Welcome to our creative corner on the web! We're Sarah and Lizzie, sisters and co-founders of our vibrant sticker and apparel shop. Our journey began amidst the uncertainty of COVID-19 when we decided to turn our passion for design and art into a small business that brings joy and color to everyday items.

Here at our shop, you'll find a diverse range of products, from uniquely designed stickers perfect for personalizing your space, to custom apparel that lets you wear your personality. We specialize in tailor-made items, ensuring that each piece reflects your individual style and preferences.

We also offer a curated selection of our creations on Etsy, making it easy for you to shop our most loved designs. Whether you're looking for something special for yourself or a thoughtful gift, we're here to help you find or create exactly what you need.

2024 Our Goal

At Sarah and Lizzie's Sticker and Apparel Shop, our goal is to infuse a bit of joy and personal flair into everyday items through our unique, custom-designed stickers and apparel. Born from a creative spark during the challenging times of COVID-19, we aim to build a business that not only celebrates individual expression but also fosters a sense of community among our customers. Whether it's through our bespoke services or our curated collections on Etsy, we strive to offer high-quality, accessible designs that resonate with our diverse clientele. By providing exceptional service and continually engaging with customer feedback, we seek to grow our platform into a beloved destination for those looking to express themselves creatively.
